Privacy Policy

Crypto Futures Calculator  ·  Last updated: March 24, 2026

Crypto Futures Calculator ("the App") is developed and maintained by Hyunjin Cho ("I", "me", or "my"). This Privacy Policy explains what information the App collects and how it is used.

Information Collection

The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. Specifically:

  • No account registration or login is required.
  • No name, email address, or contact information is collected.
  • No location data is accessed or stored.
  • No device identifiers or usage analytics are collected.
  • All calculations are performed entirely on your device.

Third-Party Services

The App fetches publicly available cryptocurrency price data from the Binance public REST API (api.binance.com). This request contains no user-identifying information — only a coin symbol is sent to retrieve its current market price. Please refer to Binance's Privacy Policy for information about their data practices.

Data Storage

Any values you enter in the App (prices, quantities, leverage) are stored locally on your device only and are never transmitted to any server.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with a revised "Last updated" date.
